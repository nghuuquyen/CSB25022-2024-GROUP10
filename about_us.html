Our Record

WINNER AWARDS

BAKERY OF THE YEAR

2023

In 2023, the small Two Worms Bakery was named "Bakery of the Year" by the Artisanal Bakers Association, recognizing its use of quality local ingredients to make delicious breads and pastries. The 3-year-old bakery's products, including their signature banana loaves and cupcakes, were especially praised for their excellent taste and texture.

CUPCAKES SHOP OF THE YEAR

2022 - 2023

The National Cupcake Association honored Two Worms Bakery with the title of "Cupcakes Shop of the Year" in August 2023 in recognition of their creative flavors, outstanding customer support, and dedication to using only the best, locally sourced ingredients. The bakery's famous Blueberry and Chocolate cupcakes have garnered a devoted customer base.

THE GOLDEN HAND

2020 - 2023

The National Bakers Association awarded Ms. Velvet Vanilla of Two Worms Bakery the prestigious "Golden Hand" in 2023. This distinguished honor celebrated Ms. Vanilla's exceptional baking talents and her ability to craft innovative, flavor-forward cupcakes. Her signature creations had earned her a reputation as one of the most talented and creative bakers in the region.
